Pudding

Description

Lassi, on the other hand, is a cool and refreshing drink. It's made with yogurt, water, and sometimes sugar. Lassi is perfect for a hot day! You can also add fruits like mango or mint for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients: In a blender, combine yogurt, water, sugar, and salt (if using).
  2. Blend: Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Add ice: Add ice cubes and blend again until the lassi is icy cold.
  4. For a sweeter lassi: You can add honey or a sweetener of your choice.
  5. For a different flavor: Try adding a ripe mango, a handful of mint leaves, or a splash of rose water.
